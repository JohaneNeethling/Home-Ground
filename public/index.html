30 YEARS EXPERIENCE

With nearly 30 years in the Construction Industry, Home Ground Developments, Paul and his son Jason, have built a strong reputation for quality and reliability specialising in Residential Construction. Our portfolio includes Prestigious Estates throughout our Home Province of Kwa Zulu - Natal:

  • Sommerset Park and Mt Edgecombe Golf Estate in Umhlanga Rocks.
  • Cotswold Downs Golf Estate , Plantations Residential Estate , Langford Country Estate , Kirtlington Park Equestrian Estate and Augusta Golf Estate in the Outer West Village of Hillcrest.
  • Currently the Greater Overberg District , including and not limited to Romansbaai Beach and Fynbos Estate

    • After 25 successful years in KwaZulu-Natal, we relocated to Gansbaai in 2024 and continue to deliver outstanding projects. In addition to being Nominated Contractors on the above Estates , we’ve completed multiple Sectional Title Property Developments as the Sole Developer and Contractor in Hillcrest Village. Our achievements have been accomplished over the past decades by adhering diligently to the basic ethics of good business practise. Starting with a thorough knowledge of our profession and the many trades related to it . Home Ground Developments insists that all who work for and with us share our values. We look forward to a potential collaboration and the opportunity to share our portfolio with you.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is our background in the construction industry?
    Home Ground Developments has nearly three decades of experience in the construction industry, with a strong track record of successful residential projects. The company was founded by Paul, a seasoned professional with a Btech QS Qualification, and is now a family-run business with his eldest son, Jason, contributing as an on-site project manager.
    Where have we completed projects?
    We have successfully completed a range of Residential Construction Projects in various prestigious Estates within Kwa Zulu Natal, including Summerset Park and Mt Edgecombe in Umhlanga Augusta Country Estate, Kirtlington Equestrian Park , Langford Country Estate, Plantations Estate and Cotswold Downs Golf Estate in Hillcrest KZN More recently the Overberg area , currently Romansbaai Beach and Fynbos Estate in Gansbaai.
    What types of projects do we specialize in?
    Home Ground Developments specializes in residential construction, having completed multiple projects on esteemed estates. We also have experience in property development, including four sectional title developments in Hillcrest Village over the past six to eight years.
    What are the recent projects completed by us?
    Our most recent projects include Romansbaai Beach and Fynbos Estate in Gansbaai, where we continue to make a positive impact in the area.
    Do we offer property development services?
    Yes, we also offer property development services. In addition to our construction expertise, we have completed four sectional title developments in Hillcrest Village and are open to exploring further property development opportunities.
